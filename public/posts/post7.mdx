<p>
  Four Gentlemen Decoction (Si Jun Zi tang) is a traditional Chinese medicinal
  formula passed down through generations. It has the effects of nourishing the
  stomach, invigorating the spleen. It is commonly used to treat chronic
  gastritis, gastroenteritis, loss of appetite, vomiting, and diarrhea.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:
  <br />
  Codonopsis Root 5 grams
  <br />
  Atractylodis Rhizome 5 grams
  <br />
  Hoelen 5 grams
  <br />
  Baked Licorice Root 2 grams
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add hot water to make tea. Boil for 5 minutes for better effects. If your
  condition is really bad, add the following:
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Amomum Fruit 5 grams
  <br />
  Costus Root 5 grams
</p>
