<p>
  Jade Windscreen Powder (Yu Ping Feng San), an ancient Chinese herbal formula
  for immune system boosting. Warm the body and regulate immune functions. This
  tea can relieve common cold, flu, inflammation, and allergic rhinitis. Best
  used for people with a white-coated tongue.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:
  <br />
  Siler Root 3 grams
  <br />
  Atractylodis Rhizome 5 grams
  <br />
  Astragalus Root 5 grams
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add 16 oz of hot water. Let it sit for 5 minutes before drinking. Boil for better effects.
</p>
