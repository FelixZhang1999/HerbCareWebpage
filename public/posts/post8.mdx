  This formula can protect and detox the liver. It is helpful for fatty liver and hepatitis.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:
  <br />
  Hawthorn berry 4g
  <br />
  Red Sage Root 4g
  <br />
  Astragalus Root 4g
  <br />
  Capillary wormwood 2g
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add 16 oz of water and boil for 5 minutes. Add flavors like sugar if you want.
