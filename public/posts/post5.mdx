<p>
  A Chinese herbal formula for immune system boosting named "Gui Zhi Tang" or
  "Cinnamon Twig Decoction". It is used to disperse cold, release the muscles,
  regulate the immuse systems. It is mainly used for headache and fever,
  cold sweat, nasal congestion and dry retching.
  It is commonly used in the clinic to treat cold, influenza,
 upper respiratory tract infection and other conditions related to immuse system.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:
  <br />
  Cinnamon Twig 5g
  <br />
  White Peony Root 4g
  <br />
  Baked Licorice Root 3g
  <br />
  Fresh Ginger 3g
  <br />
  Chinese Date 3g
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add hot water to make tea. Boil for 3 to 5 minutes for better effects. Add
  more water after the first pot of tea.
</p>
