<p>
  A Chinese herbal formula for immune system boosting named Cinnamon Twig
  Decoction (Gui Zhi Tang). It is used to disperse cold, release the muscles,
  regulate the immuse systems. It is mainly used for headache and fever, cold
  sweat, nasal congestion and dry retching. It is commonly used in the clinic to
  treat cold, influenza, upper respiratory tract infection and other conditions
  related to the immuse system.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:
  <br />
  Cinnamon Twig 5 grams
  <br />
  White Peony Root 4 grams
  <br />
  Baked Licorice Root 3 grams
  <br />
  Fresh Ginger 3 grams
  <br />
  Chinese Date 3 grams
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add 16 oz of hot water. Let it sit for 5 minutes before drinking. Boil for better effects.
</p>
