<p>
  A Chinese herbal tea recipe for healthy blood pressure, blood fat, and blood sugar.
  This tea formula is also good for fatty Liver and for losing weight.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:<br />
  Capillary wormwood 3 grams<br />
  Hawthorn berry 3 grams<br />
  Lotus leaf 3 grams<br />
  Tangerine peel 3 grams<br />
  Polygonatum odoratum 3 grams
</p>
<br />
<p>
Add hot water to make tea. Boil for 3 to 5 minutes for better effects.
Add more water after the first pot of tea. Drink for 1 to 2 months to see the effects.
</p>
