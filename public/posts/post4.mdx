<p>
  A Chinese herbal tea formula lowers high blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels. It is also good for healing fatty liver and to lose weight.
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Formula:<br />
  Capillary wormwood 3 grams<br />
  Hawthorn berry 3 grams<br />
  Lotus leaf 3 grams<br />
  Tangerine peel 3 grams<br />
  Solomon's seal 3 grams
</p>
<br />
<p>
  Add 16 oz of water and boil. Let it sit for 5 minutes before drinking. You will see the effects in 1 month.
</p>
